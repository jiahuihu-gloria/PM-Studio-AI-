# Roadmap

## MVP

Goal: prove that one-sentence product ideas can become structured, interview-ready AI product strategy workspaces.

Scope:

- One-sentence idea intake
- Sequential multi-agent workflow
- Structured PM artifacts
- AI architecture recommendation
- Evaluation dashboard
- Markdown export

## V1

Goal: support richer product discovery and evidence grounding.

Scope:

- Upload interview notes, research docs, and competitor notes
- Add RAG for source-grounded outputs
- Add artifact versioning
- Add user-editable agent outputs
- Add PDF and DOCX export

## V2

Goal: become a collaborative AI PM workspace.

Scope:

- Project history
- Team collaboration
- Commenting and review
- Custom agent workflows
- Live competitor research
- Workspace templates for product archetypes

## V3

Goal: support real product teams and enterprise use cases.

Scope:

- Integrations with Notion, Linear, Jira, and Google Docs
- Role-based access control
- Audit logs
- Evaluation datasets
- Organization-level playbooks
- Enterprise governance settings
