# Competitive Analysis

## Category Landscape

PM Studio AI sits between AI writing tools, product management software, and AI workflow platforms.

## Competitor Categories

### Horizontal AI Assistants

Examples:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini

Gap:

- Strong at drafting text
- Weak at enforcing product workflow
- No native PM artifact structure
- No visible multi-agent reasoning or evaluation layer

### Product Management Tools

Examples:

- Notion
- Linear
- Productboard
- Jira Product Discovery

Gap:

- Strong workspace and collaboration primitives
- Weak AI-native product strategy generation
- AI features often assist writing rather than running a PM workflow

### AI Workflow Builders

Examples:

- LangGraph demos
- CrewAI demos
- AutoGen demos

Gap:

- Strong technical agent orchestration
- Often weak on product UX, business framing, and portfolio-ready artifacts

## Differentiation

PM Studio AI differentiates through:

- A dedicated AI PM workflow
- Visual agent sequence
- Structured PM artifacts
- RAG/Agent architecture recommendation
- AI quality and risk evaluation
- Portfolio-ready case study framing

## Positioning

> The AI Product Manager workspace for turning rough product ideas into interview-ready product strategy systems.
